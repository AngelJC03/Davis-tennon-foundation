Resources for Families and Community Partners

At the Davis-Tennon Foundation, we are dedicated to connecting families with the tools, organizations, and support systems they need to thrive. Below, you’ll find a curated list of resources and partner organizations that address critical needs in our community.

Empowering Women and Preventing Domestic Violence

  • Rhode Island Coalition Against Domestic Violence: Comprehensive support services for survivors, including shelter, advocacy, and prevention programs.
  • Sojourner House: Offering housing, counseling, and education to individuals impacted by domestic violence.

Housing Assistance

  • Rhode Island Foundation: Initiatives and grants supporting housing stability and community development.
  • Papitto Opportunity Connection: Programs focused on providing housing resources and opportunities for underserved communities.

Student Enrichment and Education

  • Rhode Island College: Resources for students and families seeking higher education pathways and enrichment programs.
  • Central Falls Workforce Hub: Workforce training and career advancement opportunities for local students and families.

Financial and Grant Support

  • The Champlin Foundation: Funding opportunities for nonprofit organizations working to enhance community services.
  • Trailblazers Initiative by the Charter School Growth Fund: Support for innovative educational programs.

Parent Advocacy and Leadership

  • Parent Council Development: Learn about the Davis-Tennon Foundation's initiative to train parents in advocacy and leadership to better identify and address community needs.

Contact Us for Assistance

If you’re unsure where to start or need help connecting with the right resource, our team is here to assist. Contact us at Angelo.Garcia@segueifl.org for guidance and referrals.

Together, we can eliminate barriers, build connections, and create brighter futures for families across Rhode Island.
