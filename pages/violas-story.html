Viola's Story

“I grew up in Central Falls. It is the place I call home, and it is where my dreams took shape, and it also carries memories of the brutal aspects of poverty. Within that community holds so many people who have the vision, ability, and talent to shift the world, and within me is the ability to give them the tools to do so. More than awards, it is my legacy.”

A Brief Timeline of Viola Davis

Viola Davis was born and raised in South Carolina, experiencing significant challenges that shaped her resilience and determination.

She began her acting career on stage, earning critical acclaim for her performances in Broadway productions.

Davis gained widespread recognition for her role in *Doubt*, earning her first Academy Award nomination.
